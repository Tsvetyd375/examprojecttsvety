Marselisborg Memorial Park

In the beautiful surroundings
overlooking the bay of Aarhus

Between Marselisborg Castle and Aarhus bay lies the
Memorial Park in Aarhus.
Marselisborg Memorial Park
is a must-see excursion destination with
beautiful gardens and cultural and historical
elements. You can go for a walk, enjoy the
peace in one of the garden rooms,
experience blooming lushness, or exercise in
the large outdoor fitness facility. There is also
a good playground and an ice house, so you
can cool off while sunbathing on the park's
large grassy areas.

Kongevejen 100, Aarhus 8000 Denmark
