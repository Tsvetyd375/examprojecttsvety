Forest Botanical Garden

The beautiful natural oasis with around
900 different species of trees and shrubs

The garden was laid out in 1923, and
in addition to what
has been planted over time,
it also contains several majestic, old oak
and beech trees from before the garden was built.
The trees form a kind of upper floor in the
garden and contribute to the wonderful, troll-
like feel that characterizes Forsthaven.

Skovridervej 20, 8000 Aarhus C

17, Væddeløbsbanen/Carl Nielsens Vej (Aarhus Kom);
100, 103, 302, Kongevejen/Oddervej (Aarhus Kom)
